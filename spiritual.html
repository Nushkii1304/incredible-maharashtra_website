Ganapatipule

Ganpatipule is a coastal town in Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra. Due to its Ganpati mandir the town is popular Hindu pilgrimage and tourists destination.

Mahalakshmi Mandir

The temple's structure showcases a combination of Hindu and Hemadpanti architectural styles, with intricate carvings, sculptures, and ornamental work.

Trimbakeshwar Temple

The temple is located between three hills namely Brahmagiri, Nilagiri and Kalagiri. The temple has three lingas (an iconic form of Shiva) representing Shiva, Vishnu and Brahma.
